### 🌐 What is World Credit?

World Credit is a **portable trust and reputation score** built from the accounts you already use. We aggregate signals from across your digital life — social presence, payment history, work platforms, and more — into a single **0–100 World Score** that proves you're trustworthy.

Think of it as a **credit score for the internet** — but instead of just financial data, it measures *reliability*, *financial integrity*, and *social proof* across your connected accounts.

### 🔗 How It Works

```
📱 Link your accounts  →  🔍 We verify signals  →  📊 Get your World Score
```

1. **Link** — Connect platforms like Google, GitHub, LinkedIn, Facebook, and more
2. **Verify** — We analyze account age, activity patterns, and reliability signals
3. **Score** — Your World Score (0–100) reflects your verified digital trustworthiness
4. **Share** — Carry your score anywhere with embeddable badges, QR codes, or shareable links

### 🏅 Trust Tiers

| Tier | Score | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Bronze** | 1 – 19 | Getting started |
| **Silver** | 20 – 49 | Building trust |
| **Gold** | 50 – 79 | Established trust |
| **Platinum** | 80 – 100 | Exceptional trust |

### 🏗️ Score Categories

| Category | What It Measures | Example Signals |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Reliability** | Do you show up? | Calendar attendance, account age, phone verification |
| **Financial Integrity** | Are you financially responsible? | Payment disputes, on-time payments, account standing |
| **Social Proof** | Do others vouch for you? | Endorsements, vouches, social network verification |

### 🛡️ Embeddable Trust Badges

Add verified trust scores to any app or website. 4 badge styles, dark/light themes, 3 sizes.

```html
<!-- Web — 2 lines -->
<div data-worldcredit="handle" data-style="inline"></div>
<script src="https://world-credit.com/sdk/badge.js"></script>
```

```swift
// iOS — Swift Package
InlineBadge(handle: "handle")
```

```kotlin
// Android — Jetpack Compose
InlineBadge(handle = "handle")
```

```dart
// Flutter
WCInlineBadge(handle: 'handle')
```

See all badge styles live → [world-credit.com/sdk](https://world-credit.com/sdk/)
