### 🌐 What is World Credit?

World Credit is a **portable trust and reputation score** built from the accounts you already use. We aggregate signals from across your digital life — ride-sharing ratings, payment history, social presence, work platforms, and more — into a single **0–100 World Score** that proves you're trustworthy.

Think of it as a **credit score for the internet** — but instead of just financial data, it measures *reliability*, *financial integrity*, and *social proof* across 27+ platforms.

### 🔗 How It Works

```
📱 Link your accounts  →  🔍 We verify signals  →  📊 Get your World Score
```

1. **Link** — Connect platforms like Google, GitHub, LinkedIn, Venmo, Uber, Airbnb, and more
2. **Verify** — We analyze account age, ratings, activity patterns, and reliability signals
3. **Score** — Your World Score (0–100) reflects your verified digital trustworthiness
4. **Share** — Carry your score anywhere with a public badge, QR code, or shareable link

### 🏗️ Score Categories

| Category | What It Measures | Example Signals |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Reliability** | Do you show up? | Calendar attendance, ride ratings, delivery consistency |
| **Financial Integrity** | Are you financially responsible? | Payment disputes, on-time payments, account standing |
| **Social Proof** | Do others vouch for you? | Endorsements, vouches from high-trust users |

### 🛡️ Privacy First

- **You control what you link** — nothing is mandatory
- **We verify, we don't store** — signals are processed, not hoarded
- **No PII in analytics** — ever
- **Right to be forgotten** — delete your data anytime

### 📱 The App

World Credit is a **cross-platform mobile app** built with Flutter, powered by Firebase, with real-time score updates and OAuth integrations.

**Features:**
- 🔐 Google & Apple Sign-In
- 📊 Animated World Score with trust breakdown
- 🔗 27+ signal connectors (OAuth-verified)
- 📞 Phone verification via Twilio
- 🤝 Vouch system — request and give vouches
- 📈 Score history timeline with charts
- 🏷️ Shareable public profile with QR code
- 🌙 Dark & light mode

### 🚀 Vision

Every marketplace, gig platform, rental service, and community needs trust. World Credit is building the **universal trust layer** — so your reputation follows you everywhere.
